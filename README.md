# README for Blackwire NYC Website

Welcome to Blackwire NYC — your gateway to understanding the real New York City from the streets to tech labs. Blackwire is a community-driven web platform dedicated to uncovering the inner workings of NYC — from gangs and politics to cutting-edge technology.

## What You'll Find Here

- In-depth exploration of NYC's social and political communities
- Discussions about technology of all kinds: new, old, Pi projects, supercomputer setups, and more
- Coverage of cybersecurity, including small hacks, network discussions, and team accomplishments
- Daily live updates at 5 PM featuring street and subway reports on cyber security industry news and city events
- Tutorials, guides, and a future learning portal inspired by resources like W3Schools and freeCodeCamp

## Our Mission

Blackwire NYC is about learning, sharing, and supporting our city — the place that gave us everything. We aim to build knowledge, encourage community, and help make NYC great again.

## Important Guidelines

Please respect our **Code of Conduct**. Blackwire is a safe space for honest conversations about the city and technology. Remember:  
**No snitching — everything stays within Blackwire unless public safety is at risk. In such cases, report to proper authorities.**

# Code of Conduct for Blackwire NYC

### Our Commitment

Blackwire NYC is a community built on respect, trust, and shared learning. It is a space where members can discuss sensitive topics openly and safely.

### Guidelines

- **Respect Privacy:** Keep all community discussions confidential. Do not share information outside of Blackwire unless it concerns public danger.
- **No Snitching:** What is shared in Blackwire stays in Blackwire. Only report matters to authorities if there is a clear threat to public safety.
- **Be Respectful:** Treat all community members with kindness and respect, regardless of their background or views.
- **Stay Safe:** Do not promote or engage in illegal activities. Our focus is on education, awareness, and positive contributions.
- **Constructive Discussion:** Share knowledge about NYC, technology, cybersecurity, and hacking with integrity and good intentions.
- **Inclusivity:** Everyone interested in learning and supporting NYC is welcome.

Thank you for helping us maintain a respectful, trusting, and educational environment.
